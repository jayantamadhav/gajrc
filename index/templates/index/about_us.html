About Us

South Asian Research Publication (SAR Publication) is a publisher for scientific online and print journals started with collaboration with other scientific organizations, institutions, academicians and researchers. SAR Publication is keen to make itself as a leading publisher for scientific and academic journals with quality peer review and rapid publication. It is self-supporting publisher and does not depend on funding from any organization. Hence, to cover publication expenses, maintenance and operation expenses, a minimal processing fee is collected from authors and is required to make payment only after their manuscript have been accepted for publication.

Advantages 
• SAR Publication is dedicated for providing services
• Fast Acknowledgment of submitted manuscript
• Rapid peer-review process within 7 to 10 days
• Experienced and subject wise editorial board
• No article submission fees
• Low article processing charges
• Publication in 15 days
• Open access publication policy
• Authors retain copyright
• No extra page charges
